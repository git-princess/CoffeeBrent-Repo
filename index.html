Every cup brewed today reflects Elaine’s heart — supporting local communities, creating meaningful moments, and reminding people to slow down and savor something real.

Coffee Brent began with Elaine Bautista’s simple dream and deep love for coffee. What started as a small cart in Pasig City has grown into a warm community space built on passion, sincerity, and stories shared over a fresh cup.

We put the

on Coffee Brent

In the bustling streets of Pasig City, Elaine took a brave first step by opening a small coffee cart. With only a few team members and a heart full of determination, she started serving comforting classics — hot coffees, iced brews, and her early signature blends.

She poured her heart into every cup, making sure each one carried the same love and sincerity she felt at home. For Elaine, it wasn’t just about selling coffee; it was about creating moments — those quiet little pauses in the day where people could breathe, smile, and feel cared for.
